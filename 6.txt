-- Insert Products (Using Hashes)
HSET product:101 Name "Laptop" Category "Electronics" Price 800
HSET product:102 Name "Phone" Category "Electronics" Price 600
HSET product:103 Name "Shoes" Category "Fashion" Price 1200
HSET product:104 Name "Headphones" Category "Electronics" Price 900
HSET product:105 Name "Watch" Category "Accessories" Price 700

-- Maintain a Set for category indexing
SADD category:Electronics 101 102 104
SADD category:Fashion 103
SADD category:Accessories 105

-- Retrieve Specific Product by ProductID
HGETALL product:101

SMEMBERS category:Electronics

HGETALL product:101
HGETALL product:102
HGETALL product:104

-- List Products in Price Range (500 - 1000)
ZADD price_index 800 101
ZADD price_index 600 102
ZADD price_index 1200 103
ZADD price_index 900 104
ZADD price_index 700 105

ZRANGEBYSCORE price_index 500 1000

HGETALL product:101
HGETALL product:102
HGETALL product:104
HGETALL product:105

-- Update Product Price
HSET product:101 Price 850

ZADD price_index 850 101

-- Delete a Product
DEL product:103

SREM category:Fashion 103
ZREM price_index 103

-- Get Only Product Name
HGET product:101 Name

-- Get Multiple Fields of a Product
HMGET product:101 Name Price

-- Products in Multiple Categories (Union)
SUNION category:Electronics category:Accessories

-- Products Common in Two Categories (Intersection)
SINTER category:Electronics category:Accessories

-- Products in Electronics but NOT in Fashion
SDIFF category:Electronics category:Fashion

-- Top 3 Most Expensive Products
ZREVRANGE price_index 0 2

-- Cheapest Product
ZRANGE price_index 0 0

-- Increase Price (Numeric Update)
HINCRBY product:101 Price 50
